ABOUT
THE LEGEND

Shri Varthaman Abhinandan is an Indian Air Force fighter pilot who, during the 2019 Indiaâ€“Pakistan standoff, was held captive in Pakistan for 60 hours after his aircraft was shot down in an aerial dogfight. He was awarded with Vir Chakra, third highest wartime gallantry award, by President Ram Nath Kovind for downing a PAF F-16 with his MiG-21 Bison. He showed conspicuous courage, demonstrated gallantry in the face of the enemy while disregarding personal safety and displayed exceptional sense of duty. His distinctive style of mustache also gained massive popularity in the media following the incident.He was promoted to the rank of Group captain from the rank of Wing Commander on November, 2021.
